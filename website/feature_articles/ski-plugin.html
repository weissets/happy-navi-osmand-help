OsmAnd Ski Maps is a plugin allowing you to navigate at winter sports locations. It provides you with a full map of ski pistes, ski lift markers and even colored lines indicating the complexity of each track.

How to use it

Android

Download the plugin, then use the menu item 'Configure Map' to switch the winter and ski style on. Then find your desired area on the map and enjoy the info about the ski tracks including their complexity, location and infrastructure around.

iOS

To activate ski maps, you need to go to the Maps & Resources menu, then choose Plugins. Enable the Ski map plugin and return to the map. Now, tap the map button on the left and select the Map type menu. Change it to Ski map. That's it. Now you can view the maps you've downloaded in the ski mode. The rest of the world will have the info provided in the World map and shown in ski map style.

OsmAnd Ski maps plugin works perfectly well with Contour lines plugin and the hillshade maps. Contour lines show the heights of a location and the hillshade maps provide visualization of terrain which may be of great help at sports locations.

Adding contour lines and hillshade

Android

You need to go to Settings – Manage map files – All downloads and choose Contour lines or Hillshade overlay in the menu. After that, get the map you need. You can download both map types for one location to combine them.

OsmAnd Ski maps provide you with helpful info on winter sports locations. Besides that, you can see the nearby mountain peaks. The plugin also allows you to find the location of aerialway stations.

iOS

To combine maps with contour lines or hillshade, you have to download the contour lines or hillshade maps of the area you need to view together with the regular map. Then just go to Maps & Resources - Plugins and turn the Contour lines plugin on. That will allow you to view both map in the ski map style, contour lines or hillshade optionally together. To turn the hillshade off and leave just the ski map and contour lines, go to the Map menu and disable Hillshade.
